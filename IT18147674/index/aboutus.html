About Us



We have over 20 years experience of owning, repairing, restoring and running Rover P6 cars in all variants, 2000, 2200 & 3500, saloon and estate, both as everyday and show condition cars. Our current fleet includes an everyday user 1966 Rover 2000, and also a 1972 Rover 2000TC export, 1975 Rover 2200 Auto, 1972 Rover 3500 Estoura (estate) and of course, the last P6 off the production line 'VVC 700S', the avocado Rover 3500S.
